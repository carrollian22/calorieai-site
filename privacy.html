Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Introduction

CalorieAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and authentication credentials
  • Health & Fitness Data: Weight, height, age, biological sex, activity level, exercise frequency, daily step count, and body composition goals
  • Food Logs: Text descriptions of food consumed, meal times, calorie estimates, and macronutrient breakdowns (protein, carbohydrates, fat)
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, and preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the CalorieAI service
  • Calculate personalized calorie targets and macronutrient recommendations
  • Process food descriptions using AI to estimate nutritional content
  • Generate insights and coaching recommendations
  • Communicate with you about your account and provide customer support
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our services

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate CalorieAI:

  • Supabase: Cloud database provider that stores your account information, food logs, weight data, and app preferences. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • OpenAI: AI service provider that processes your food descriptions to estimate calories and macronutrients. Food text is sent to OpenAI's API but is not used to train their models per OpenAI's API data usage policy.
  • Google Gemini: AI service provider used to process food descriptions for nutritional estimation. Food text is sent to Google's Gemini API. Data sent via the API is not used to train Google's models per Google's API data usage policy.
  • RevenueCat: In-app purchase and subscription management service that handles billing for premium features.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share data with the service providers listed above, and only to the extent necessary to provide CalorieAI's features. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the app's Settings screen. Deleting your account will permanently remove all your personal data, food logs, weight history, and preferences from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest, secure authentication, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

CalorieAI is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using CalorieAI, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or through the app. Continued use of CalorieAI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
